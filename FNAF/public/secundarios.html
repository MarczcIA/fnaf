Extras & Others

Discover all minor, secret, cameo, AI, and easter egg characters in the FNAF universe: Dee Dee, Phone Guy, HandUnit, Glitchtrap, Candy Cadet, spirits, Old Man Consequences, and more!

Dee Dee

Secondary / Ultimate Custom Night

Randomly appears in UCN and summons other animatronics.

Dee Dee

Secondary / Ultimate Custom Night

Description

Randomly enters the office and adds new animatronics to the night, making it harder.

Weaknesses

  • Cannot be prevented or countered in UCN.

Games appeared in

  • Ultimate Custom Night

Trivia

  • Has a rare "sister" variant named XOR in UCN.

Phone Guy

Secondary / FNAF 1-3

Provides tips and lore via voicemails in the early games.

Phone Guy

Secondary / FNAF 1-3

Description

Leaves a recorded message at the start of each night, giving hints, story, and sometimes dark secrets.

Weaknesses

  • Audio can be muted, but has no direct effect on gameplay.

Games appeared in

  • FNAF 1, 2, 3, UCN

Trivia

  • Voice acted by Scott Cawthon, the series creator.

HandUnit

AI / Sister Location

Automated voice assistant in Sister Location. Provides guidance and comic relief.

HandUnit

AI / Sister Location

Description

Delivers instructions, sarcastic remarks, and occasionally insults the player.

Weaknesses

  • Cannot be turned off.

Games appeared in

  • Sister Location, Help Wanted

Trivia

  • Voiced by Andy Field.

Glitchtrap

Virus / Help Wanted

Digital virus version of William Afton, main antagonist of FNAF VR.

Glitchtrap

Virus / Help Wanted

Description

Appears as a humanoid bunny. Represents Afton's consciousness in the digital world, manipulates the player.

Weaknesses

  • Infects code; no in-game defense.

Games appeared in

  • Help Wanted, Security Breach (cameo), Tales from the Pizzaplex (books)

Trivia

  • First appearance of Afton as a virus, hints at new threats.

Candy Cadet

Secondary / FNAF 6

Robot vending machine. Tells dark, mysterious stories in FNAF 6.

Candy Cadet

Secondary / FNAF 6

Description

Dispenses candy and cryptic stories after being used multiple times.

Weaknesses

  • Can’t be interacted with after stories are done.

Games appeared in

  • FNAF 6, UCN

Trivia

  • His stories are fan theories fuel!

Old Man Consequences

Secondary / UCN

8-bit fishing minigame character, originally from FNAF World, reappears in UCN.

Old Man Consequences

Secondary / UCN

Description

Forces the player into a fishing minigame in UCN. May hold lore secrets.

Weaknesses

  • Quick reflexes to win the minigame.

Games appeared in

  • FNAF World, UCN

Trivia

  • Suspected to be a representation of Cassidy's spirit.

Spirits (Lost Children)

Ghost / All Games

Haunt the animatronics. Represented by the children’s spirits.

Spirits (Lost Children)

Ghost / All Games

Description

The souls of murdered children who possess the animatronics and drive the main FNAF story.

Weaknesses

  • Cannot be fought; freed by completing secret endings.

Games appeared in

  • FNAF 1, 2, 3, 4, Sister Location, FNAF 6, UCN, etc.

Trivia

  • The most important non-visible characters in the series.

Shadow Dee Dee (XOR)

Secret / UCN

Shadow version of Dee Dee, appears on 50/20 mode in UCN to add chaos.

Shadow Dee Dee (XOR)

Secret / UCN

Description

Appears only during maximum chaos mode, makes the night even harder by summoning secret animatronics.

Weaknesses

  • Cannot be countered; is a random, rare event.

Games appeared in

  • Ultimate Custom Night

Trivia

  • XOR means “exclusive or” in programming, fitting for her secretive nature.

RWQFSFASXC (Shadow Bonnie)

Secret / UCN / FNAF 2

Glitchy, black silhouette of Bonnie; can cause hallucinations and secrets.

RWQFSFASXC (Shadow Bonnie)

Secret / UCN / FNAF 2

Description

Rare hallucination in FNAF 2, playable in FNAF World, powerful secret in UCN.

Weaknesses

  • Random event, can’t be prevented.

Games appeared in

  • FNAF 2, UCN, FNAF World

Trivia

Fredbear Plush

Plush / FNAF 4

Description

Appears to “talk” in FNAF 4’s minigames, giving hints and warnings about the animatronics.

Helpy

Helper / FNAF 6

Description

Tape Girl

Guide / Help Wanted

Mysterious voice in FNAF VR: Help Wanted, leaves audio logs about Glitchtrap and the digital virus.

Tape Girl

Guide / Help Wanted

Description

Her logs reveal the dangers of Glitchtrap and the corrupted VR game code.

Weaknesses

  • Not a threat; helps players understand the lore.

Games appeared in

  • Help Wanted

Trivia

Security Puppet

Secondary / FNAF 6

Alternate form of The Puppet, seen in FNAF 6 minigame, represents Charlotte’s spirit.

Security Puppet

Secondary / FNAF 6

Description

Appears in a tragic minigame, tries to protect the daughter of Henry Emily, ultimately failing and becoming The Puppet.

Weaknesses

  • Story-only, no direct threat.

Games appeared in

  • FNAF 6, UCN (cameo)

Trivia

  • The minigame is among the most emotional in the series.
