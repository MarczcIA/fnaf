Explore all human characters in the FNAF universe: Afton family, security guards, main characters, kids, protagonists, antagonists, and more!

William Afton

Antagonist / Founder

The main villain, creator of the animatronics, child murderer, known as Purple Guy and Springtrap.

William Afton

Antagonist / Founder

Description

Co-founder of Fazbear Entertainment, responsible for the Missing Children Incident and much of the FNAF lore's tragedy. Becomes Springtrap after being trapped in a suit.

Aliases

  • Purple Guy, Springtrap, Scraptrap, Glitchtrap (virus), Burntrap, The Man Behind the Slaughter.

Games appeared in

  • FNAF 2, 3, 4 (minigames), Sister Location, FNAF 6, UCN, Help Wanted, Security Breach, Ruin (as Mimic/virus)

Trivia

  • Father of the Afton family. One of gaming's most iconic villains.
